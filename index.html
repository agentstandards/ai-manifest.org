Why (Optional Reference)

  • Discoverable: standard .well-known location.
  • Interoperable: aligns with OpenAPI/JSON Schema; compatible with MCP and community agents.json.
  • Verifiable: points to JWKS for signatures, rotation and revocation.

Best Practices

  • Path: /.well-known/ai.json; Content-Type: application/json; GET/HEAD.
  • Version: manifest_version, ETag/Last-Modified, max-age=3600.
  • URLs: absolute HTTPS for OpenAPI/Schema/CRL/JWKS.
  • Trust: expose auth.jwks_uri, use kid, announce rotation windows (≥7 days), timestamp revocations.
  • Robots: allow /.well-known/ai.json; other sensitive pages noindex.
